# Business registration YMYL rewrite notes

Session learning from rewriting `khi-nao-can-dang-ky-kinh-doanh` for Vietnamese SEO/YMYL.

## Problem pattern
- Article had correct broad intent but felt like AI-generated framework content.
- Repeated filler after each heading made long article feel shallow:
  - `Khi áp dụng, đừng hỏi...`
  - `Ở bước X...`
  - `Một mẹo thực tế...`
- Legal/registration article lacked direct answer, snippet table, and source placement near claims.

## Better pattern
1. Open with `Câu trả lời nhanh` in first screen.
2. Add table with rows: situation / temporary conclusion / source or check / action.
3. Use official sources near relevant claims:
   - Nghị định 168/2025/NĐ-CP, effective 01/07/2025, from `vanban.chinhphu.vn`.
   - `dangkykinhdoanh.gov.vn` and household-business registration guide.
   - `gdt.gov.vn`/`mof.gov.vn` for tax/invoice context.
4. Label Thư Viện Pháp Luật/MISA/EasyInvoice as interpretation/reference, not primary law.
5. Replace generic sections with concrete Vietnam scenarios:
   - Zalo/Facebook selling.
   - freelancer job vs B2B contract.
   - selling digital templates.
   - invoice and acceptance documents.
   - conditional industries.
   - separating personal and business cash flow.
6. Target long-tail moat rather than fighting broad legal sites:
   - `freelancer có cần đăng ký kinh doanh không`
   - `solo founder có cần đăng ký kinh doanh không`
   - `bán dịch vụ online có cần đăng ký kinh doanh`
   - `khách công ty yêu cầu hóa đơn cá nhân làm sao`

## Verification markers
- Fast answer present.
- Snippet table present.
- Effective date present when citing new decree.
- No repeated filler strings.
- No invented article/clause numbers.
- Sources present both near claims and in source section.
